About US

Welcome to SV Eggs, a family-owned business located near Rajahmundry, Andhra Pradesh. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the freshest, high-quality eggs straight from our poultry farm. Our hens are raised in a stress-free environment with plenty of space to roam around. Our team of experienced farmers takes great care of our hens, ensuring that they are always healthy and happy. We believe that happy hens lay the best eggs! We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and the welfare of our animals. Our farm is powered by solar energy, and we use eco-friendly practices to reduce our environmental impact.

At SV Eggs, we believe that everyone should have access to fresh, healthy, and delicious eggs. That's why we offer a wide range of egg products, including free-range, organic, and omega-3 enriched eggs. We also offer bulk orders to local businesses and restaurants.

We are proud to be a part of the local community and aim to provide our customers with the best possible experience. Our team is always ready to answer your questions and help you find the perfect egg product for your needs.
